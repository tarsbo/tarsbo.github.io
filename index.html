WORK

Fifth Business

Re-designed book cover and dust jacket for the novel Fifth Business by Robertson Davies. Visually references the stone, an important symbolic element within the book.

Ocean Quotes

Typography-based quote book showcasing hand-made, paper coiled characters that emulate wavy/watery forms of the ocean.

The Table

Rebrand for the Ottawa-based vegetarian restaurant, The Table. Project included an updated logo and website that highlights the vast selection of gourmet vegetarian cuisine.

Tiffany & Co.

Print, social media, and web campaign focussing on the theme of engagement, and most prominently, their The Tiffany engagement ring..

Rolling Stone

Magazine article about rapper, Nicki Minaj, who stands out from the grid.

Hozier

Magazine layout design for an interview with Irish singer, Hozier.

Alice Cooper

Illustration of a rock-and-roll legend. Created in a style inspired by that of artist, Pablo Lobato, using simple 2-D shapes.

ABOUT

Hi, I'm Tara! I'm an Ottawa-based graphic designer, with a passion for the creative process. I have a love for all things design-related, but my specialties include illustration, branding and typography. While I love design, my other interests include music, animals and spending time with friends and family.
